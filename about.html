Operation Teams

What is Operation Teams?

Operation Education is a plan to create a website filled with free, online learning resources for students. Unlike other sources, we will not require any subscriptions whatsoever, meaning anyone, anywhere in the world, will have access to the resources provided by this site at any time. 


Resources may include a variety of things as the site develops. However, a guaranteed resource will be several full PDFs of text books, approved by some of the highest standard exam boards in the United Kingdom, such as AQA.

Why do we need your help?

In order to create the PDF text books, I have several thousand images of pages from text books that can be compiled into one PDF per book. In order to make the process possible, each image will be renamed its page number before being compiled with Adobe Acrobat DC, as to make rearranging as easy as possible.


However, renaming thousands of images based on their page number will take forever - on my own.


If we have ten, twenty or even more people renaming images, however, the images can be split amongst all the team, saving vast amount of time.
